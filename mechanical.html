MECHANICAL SERVICES SPOTLIGHT: VIRTUAL DESIGN AND CONSTRUCTION

We use the latest 3D scanning and modeling technology to give you an unprecedented look at your project before construction begins.

Advantages of 3D Modeling

  • Project Proposals: Using 3D Models for your project will give you the most accurate quotes. This is because the model is using the exact measurements needed to complete the project.

  • Documentation and Fabrication: 3D models are transformed into 2D drawings with the correct specifications. This allows for the majority of the project to be prefabricated and it reduces the total costs of the project.

  • Reduce the Amount of Rework: Virtual mechanical design and construction significantly reduces the amount field modifications. This improves the speed of the project and reduces project costs.
